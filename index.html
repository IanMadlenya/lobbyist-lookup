  What is this?

Lobbyist Lookup lets users search filings from House.gov to assist determining gift receiving eligibility.

Lobbyist Gift Ban Amendment

5 C.F.R. Part 2635 - RIN 3209-AA04 - Comments

proposed by the Office of Government Ethics (OGE) will extend the lobbyist gift ban to all federal employees.
The lobbyist gift ban prevents the acceptance of gifts of over $25 in value from unauthorized sources.

Lobbyist Lookup was made during Northern Virginia National Day of Civic Hacking (NDOCH) at National Science Foundation (NSF) in Arlington, VA.
It addresses the NSF Ethics Challenge of creating a way for federal employees to lookup lobbying status of an individual or organization.
Constituents and businesses may also find utility in using Lobbyist Lookup to be better informed on lobbyist statuses.
Lobbyist Lookup's source code is freely available on GitHub. All filing data is available to the public at House.gov.
