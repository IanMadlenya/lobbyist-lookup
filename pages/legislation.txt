Lobbyist Gift Ban Amendment
5 C.F.R. Part 2635
http://www.oge.gov/Laws-and-Regulations/OGE-Regulations/5-C-F-R--Part-2635---Standards-of-ethical-conduct-for-employees-of-the-executive-branch/
RIN 3209-AA04
http://www.reginfo.gov/public/do/eAgendaViewRule?pubId=201104&RIN=3209-AA04

Proposal by the Office of Government Ethics (OGE) to extend the lobbyist gift ban to all federal employees.
The lobbyist gift ban prevents the acceptance of gifts of over $25 in value from unauthorized sources.

Some unauthorized sources include registered lobbyists and registered lobbying organizations.

This app is meant for reference and is not legal advice. You are responsible for accurately determining gift eligibility through multiple means besides this app.
