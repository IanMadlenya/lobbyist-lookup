Lobbyist Gift Ban Amendment
5 C.F.R. Part 2635 - RIN 3209-AA04 - Comments
proposed by the Office of Government Ethics (OGE) will extend the lobbyist gift ban to all federal employees.
The lobbyist gift ban prevents the acceptance of gifts of over $25 in value from unauthorized sources.
